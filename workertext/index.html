Using Monte Carlo Method to estimate Pi value

Pin-jia (Paul) Lu, Ruei-Shan Lu

Abstract

Utilizing the computational power of modern computers to approximate the value of œÄ has long been an interesting scientific research topic. This study employs the Monte Carlo method in conjunction with a random number generator to indirectly obtain an approximation of œÄ. The approach and spirit of this process are similar to the famous Chinese folktale "Cao Chong Weighing the Elephant." Our findings indicate that with a sufficient number of random points, such as 10 million, the approximation of œÄ can be accurate to the fifth decimal place. To accelerate the computation, this study also employs the Worker technology in Web APIs to achieve parallel processing.
